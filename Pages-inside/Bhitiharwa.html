  1. Home

Description

The places where the footsteps of the Father of the Nation Mahatma Gandhi lay have become included in the historical memories and Bhitiharwa Gandhi Ashram in West Champaran is one of them.It is believed that Bapu started the Satyagraha movement from this ashram in West Champaran and also established the first basic school in the country for employable education here.Bhitiharwa Ashram represents an important stage in the emotional and social life associated with Bapu's life and many such things will be seen here which are priceless in themselves.
